Carrying the Legacy Forward

See how our students bring the Morehouse model of brotherhood and excellence to communities nationwide.

We don't just educate students

we mentor and inspire the next generation to pursue post-secondary education and envision a future without limits. Through our Get On The Bus (GOTB) program, we travel beyond our campus to connect with young minds in cities like Chicago, Los Angeles, Orlando, Washington DC, and Philadelphia, bringing with us the Morehouse model of brotherhood, excellence, and empowerment. In these spaces, we don’t just talk about college—we live it, demonstrating through mentorship, workshops, and real conversation what it means to be a leader, an innovator, and a man of distinction. Since 1867, Morehouse has been a destination for those who seek not only an education, but a legacy. Today, we carry that legacy on the road, showing students everywhere that their potential is boundless and their future starts here.
